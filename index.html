About Me

I am a full-stack web developer looking for an entry-level position!

I am passionate about data analysis and its impact on user experience. I was born and raised in Michigan and currently live in Charleston, SC. I worked as a school psychologist for eight years advocating for data-based decision making as well as research-based interventions for students who struggled in the school environment. In order to be successful in life, I believe you have to have grit! When programming, you must possess the motivation and perseverance to achieve the end goal. You have to put in the effort to overcome the obstacles and challenges presented. In addition, you need a true passion for technology and the ability to keep up with latest frameworks, languages, trends, and patterns. I am gritty and enjoy rolling up my sleeves and getting to work, whether it's training for a marathon, paddle boarding in the Low Country, building furniture in my workshop, or creating functional and beautiful websites. Coding is a challenging and creative field, one that leads to a never ending journey of learning.
